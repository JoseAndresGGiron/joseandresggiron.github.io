Target Audience

Nuestra audiencia son todas aquellas personas que deseen estar preparados para la condiciones del clima ya sean residentes de ID o turistas.

  • Status: Single, Married or Other
  • Age: 20-60 approx
  • Education: Higher education
  • Most of user will be accessing the site through mobile devices such as smartphones or tablets.

Personas

Andres
He is 23 years old he is from Guatemala. He is planning to move to Idaho to continue his college studies. Previously, Jose has traveled to the United States but he has never known snow, Andres wants to be prepared for the weather conditions while studying in Idaho so he wants to search on the internet for reliable information regarding the weather

Scarlett
She is a few weeks away from getting married. She lives in Provo, Utah which is relatively close to Idaho. She would like to plan her honeymoon in Idaho to see that state and celebrate her wedding. She wants to be sure of the weather conditions to plan where to go during her honeymoon.

Scenarios

  • It has been snowing more than ever. Should I buy winter tires for my car?
  • I've never traveled to Idaho, I have no idea what the weather is like. What kind of clothes should I pack? What kind of activities will I do?
  • I am planning my vacation. Where can I find the best landscapes in Idaho during my vacation?
  • Susana has 3 children but none of them have swum. If the weather is hot this weekend, she would like to go to a pool with them. She wants to be sure that the weather will be hot to go swimming with her children.
  • Peter suffers from asthma but would like to go hiking this weekend. He does not want to put his life at risk and that is why he wants to seek accurate information
